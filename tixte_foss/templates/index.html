Come one, come all!

Tixte FOSS lets anyone trivially set up their own hub. Tixte FOSS can run on anything from Heroku to industrial servers due to it's Python/Flask backend. All you need to run Tixte FOSS is hosting & a collection of games to satisfy your users!

You know you're safe.

Tixte FOSS has removed all of Tixte's tracking and services, letting you assure your users privacy. You can customize your databases and have full control. Tixte FOSS also lets you change your Tixte instance as much as you want to fit your needs.

Your Games. Our Hub. Now Open Source.

Setting up gaming platforms used to be hard, now its easy. Tixte FOSS lets you trivially set up an online gaming platform. By editing a few configuration files your hub can be up within minutes.
